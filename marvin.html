My Story

The honest version.

The story most people skip. But the one that actually explains who I am.

I didn't start with a grand plan. I started with curiosity — the kind that keeps you up at night searching, testing, building broken things and wondering why they're broken. That's where it all began. A young person in Nairobi, genuinely fascinated by how the internet worked and hungry to be a part of making it better.

The early days were humbling. I made ugly designs. I wrote clunky code. I sent emails that nobody replied to. But I kept going, because the process itself was rewarding. Every mistake was a lesson. Every project — no matter how small — taught me something I couldn't have learned any other way.

"I've learned more from doing than from anything else. Real work taught me what real clients need, and what real impact actually looks like."

Over time, something shifted. The work got better. The feedback got warmer. The projects got bigger. And I realised that what I was building wasn't just a skill set — it was a reputation. A track record of showing up, doing the work, and leaving things better than I found them.

Today, I work with businesses, entrepreneurs, and individuals across Kenya and beyond — helping them show up powerfully online, communicate their value clearly, and build digital presences that actually do what they're supposed to do: grow the business, attract the right people, and make a lasting impression.

That's the journey. And it's still very much in progress. I'm not standing still, I'm not resting on anything, and I'm as hungry now as I was at the beginning — maybe more so, because now I know what's possible.
